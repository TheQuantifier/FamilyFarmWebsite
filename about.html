About Roundelay® Farm

Roundelay Farm is a family farm in the beautiful Highland Rim of the Cumberland Plateau in TN. Roundelay refers to music, poetry, and dance. To us, Roundelay represents elegance with down-home simplicity, community, and harmony with the cycles of life! We are committed to nurturing both land and community through the following guiding principles:

  • Biblical stewardship of land and resources
  • Regenerative agriculture practices
  • Management-Intensive Grazing (MIG)
  • Adaptive Multi-Paddock (AMP) Grazing – a climate-resilient method featured in this CNN video

We take inspiration from Polyface Farm, a leader in sustainable farming, as well as the book The Omnivore’s Dilemma by Michael Pollan.
