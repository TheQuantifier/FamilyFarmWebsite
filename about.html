About Roundelay® Farm

Our story & values

Roundelay Farm is a family farm in the beautiful Highland Rim of the Cumberland Plateau in Tennessee. “Roundelay” refers to music, poetry, and dance— to us, it represents elegance with down‑home simplicity, community, and harmony with the cycles of life. We’re committed to nurturing both land and community through the following guiding principles:

  • Biblical stewardship of land and resources
  • Regenerative agriculture practices
  • Management‑Intensive Grazing (MIG)
  • Adaptive Multi‑Paddock (AMP) Grazing — a climate‑resilient method featured in this CNN video

We take inspiration from Polyface Farm, a leader in sustainable farming, as well as the book The Omnivore’s Dilemma by Michael Pollan.
